About Me

Five fascinating professional passions about New Product Development, Statistical Analysis, Economic Development, Dynamic Geography Information System, and Teaching allow for the successful development, management, and communication of complex business models and strategies.

Passions for multi-culture, history, geography, gourmet food, and people provide an excellent background for leadership.

Whenever I think new product development, I envision dynamic mapping in a web-environment to display the statistical differential between potential and real demand; between existing and possible marketing strategies; and between exiting and potential traffic flow. Full-stack web development talents combined together with a sound business background and an intimate geographic information system experience lead to the most interesting marketing strategies.
