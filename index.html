About this site

This site is composed of a template and representative projects. Template was acquired through Theme Forest, then modified to my needs. Projects were completed individually or in teams. All sections include both engineering and reverse engineering efforts.

Not knowing how I wanted my porfolio site to look like, I reluctantly decided to acquire a template. Template proved to become a wonderful reverse engineering exercises that showed a novice like myself what-to-do and what-not-to-do in web-design. In particular, I reviewed multiple files of CSS and JavaScript codes in order to understand the code implementation. The principal JavaScript file encompassed over 2,000 lines, or an extravagant number of line to decipher.

Individual and group projects include every aspects of developement covered during the bootcamp. Individual projects include revised projects completed prior to the camp, homework, and research activities. The difficulty was to encompass in a efficient and effective environment a diversified set of skills and experiences.

Please, note that site's sections are still being developped, as my cohort learns more technics. Some functions are not functional at this point.
